# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or
invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression,
level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal
appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness
- Being respectful of differing opinions
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback
- Taking responsibility and learning from mistakes

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks, trolling, or insulting comments
- Publishing private information without consent
- Any conduct inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in project spaces and public spaces when an individual is representing the project.

## Reporting

To report conduct issues, contact project maintainers through a private channel.
